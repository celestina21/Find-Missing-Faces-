Welcome to Find Missing Faces

Our Mission

By harnessing the power of image recognition technology, we aim to reunite families and communities.

Through this innovative technology, user-contributed photos are analysed for faces potentially matching those of reported missing persons, aiding in search efforts and providing hope and support. We are dedicated to creating a platform where every uploaded image has the potential to make a meaningful difference, bridging gaps and bringing closure to those in need. Together, we can empower our community to play an active role in solving cases and making a positive impact in the lives of others.

How YOU Can Help

Scroll through the profiles of missing persons reported to this website in "Search Profiles", or search for a profile with a specific name that comes to mind.

If any person looks familiar to someone you may have seen and captured a photo of, go to "Identify Someone" to submit the image for processing.

The top most likely matches will be given, if found.

You may choose to notify the close contacts of any matching persons, up to your discretion.

How WE Can Help You

Go to "Add Missing Person" to submit a profile.

After submitting the profile, you are given a unique profile key. Please keep it for verification in case you want to make edits to the profile in future.

*ONLY share this key with trusted individuals.

The mobile number of the close contact entered in the profile will be contacted if any users found matches to the person whose profile you submitted and chooses to notify.
